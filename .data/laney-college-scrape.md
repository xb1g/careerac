### Confirmed UC Berkeley Transfer Information (from Laney UC System Advising Sheet)

Source: https://laney.edu/hubfs/UC%20system%202024-25.pdf

**UC Transfer Admission Requirements:**
1. Complete 60 semester units of UC transferable college credit with a minimum 2.4 GPA (2.8 for non-residents)
2. Complete two transferable college courses (3 units each) in English composition
3. Complete one transferable college course (3 units) in Mathematical Concepts and Quantitative Reasoning
4. Complete four transferable college courses (3 units each) chosen from at least two of: arts/humanities, social/behavioral sciences, physical/biological sciences

**Laney Courses Satisfying UC English Composition (2 courses required):**
- ENGL C1000 (was ENGL 001A) — Academic Reading and Writing
- ENGL C1001 (was ENGL 005) — Critical Thinking and Writing

**Laney Courses Satisfying UC Mathematical Concepts:**
- MATH 1, 2, 3A, 3B, 3C, 3E, 3F, 11, 15, 16A, 16B
- STAT C1000 (was MATH 013)

**Six UC campuses offer Transfer Admission Guarantee (TAG):**
- UC Davis, UC Irvine, UC Merced, UC Riverside, UC Santa Barbara, UC Santa Cruz
- TAG is NOT available for UC Berkeley or UCLA

**UC Berkeley Specific Programs:**
- **Starting Point Mentorship Program (SPMP):** Open to CA community college students; provides guidance from current UC Berkeley transfer students
- **Transfer Alliance Project (TAP):** Partnership with CCs for major prep

### Confirmed UC Berkeley Major Preparation — Chemical Biology B.S. (2024-2025)

Source: https://assist.org/transfer/results/preview?agreement=79&... (2024-2025 Chemical Biology B.S. agreement, Laney College to UC Berkeley)

**MATH 1A + MATH 1B + MATH 53** → Satisfies Chemistry/Mathematical Sciences preparation for Chemical Biology B.S.

**ENGL 001A** → Satisfies English Composition (R&C) requirement (same as UC Berkeley's English R1A)

**AP Credit Equivalencies (from UC Berkeley ASSIST page):**
- Score 4 or 5 on AP English Language and Composition → Satisfies ENGLISH R1A
- Score 3+ on AP Calculus AB → Satisfies MATH 1A
- Score 4 or 5 on AP Calculus BC → Satisfies MATH 1A + MATH 1B
- Score 5 on AP Calculus BC → Satisfies MATH 1A + MATH 1B + MATH 53

## Recommended Verification Steps

1. **For UC Berkeley:** Go to https://www.assist.org, select "Laney College" as sending institution, "UC Berkeley" as receiving, and navigate by major or department for the current year (2025-2026).
2. **For CSU GE Breadth:** Use the Cal-GETC 2025-2026 form at https://www.csueastbay.edu/aps/cccge2526/laney-college-cal-getc-25-26.pdf as a checklist; verify each course with ASSIST.
3. **For specific CSU campuses:** ASSIST allows filtering by campus. Check CSU East Bay (closest to Oakland) and other campuses separately.
4. **For CTE/career courses:** Contact Laney counseling or the target university's career/technical credit evaluator.
5. **For nursing:** Contact the specific CSU School of Nursing directly, as nursing articulation agreements are campus-specific.
