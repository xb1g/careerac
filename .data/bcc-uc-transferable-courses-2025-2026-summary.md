## Understanding the Columns

- **Cal-GETC Areas**: California GETC designation (1-7, where 1=English Comm, 2=Math, 3=Arts/Humanities, 4=Social/Behavioral Sciences, 5=Biological/Physical Sciences, 6=Language, 7=Arts/Performance)
- **IGETC Areas**: IGETC designation (1=English Comm, 2=Math, 3=Arts/Humanities, 4=Social/Behavioral Sciences, 5=Biological/Physical Sciences w/lab, 6=Language, 7=Arts/Performance)
- **Semester Units**: Number of semester units transferable
- **UC Areas**: Which of the 7 UC requirement areas this course satisfies (E/M/H/B/S)

## Credit Limitations

- PHYS 3A/3B and PHYS 4A/4B/4C combined: maximum credit, 1 series
- BIOL 10, BIOL 25, BIOL 25A combined: maximum credit, 1 course
- No credit for BIOL 10, BIOL 25, or BIOL 25A if taken after BIOL 1A
- BUS 2 and BUS 7 combined: maximum credit, 1 course
- SPAN 2A and SPAN 22A combined: maximum credit, 1 course
- SPAN 2B and SPAN 22B combined: maximum credit, 1 course
- CIS 5 and CIS 6 combined: maximum credit, 1 course
- HONORS and regular versions of the same course: duplicate credit not awarded
- Credit awarded only to first course completed with grade of C or better
